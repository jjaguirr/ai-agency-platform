## Key Strategic Changes

### Target Market Transformation
- **From**: C-suite executives only
- **To**: Ambitious professionals (10x larger market)
  - Entrepreneurs building businesses
  - Creators building personal brands
  - Consultants building practices
  - Ambitious professionals climbing careers

### Personality & Communication Shift
- **From**: Formal, corporate business assistant
- **To**: Premium-casual conversational EA
- **Core Positioning**: Premium quality assistance with approachable, casual personality
- **Communication Style**: Friendly, motivational, contextually sophisticated
- **Interaction Examples**: 
  - Old: "I have identified three workflow optimization opportunities"
  - New: "Hey, noticed you're spending a lot of time on those client emails - want me to draft some templates?"
  - Board meeting prep: "Let's get you prepped for this board meeting - I've pulled competitive intel and created talking points that'll make you look brilliant"
  - Social strategy: "Your LinkedIn engagement is down 15% - want to brainstorm some content that'll get your audience fired up?"

### Feature Prioritization Evolution
- **From**: Business operations focus
- **To**: Personal brand + career building focus
- **Primary**: Social media management, networking, personal brand building
- **Secondary**: Business operations and workflow automation

### Pricing Strategy
- **Market Position**: Premium-but-approachable between basic AI tools ($39/month) and human EAs ($3000+/month)
- **Value Proposition**: Executive-level outcomes through friendly, accessible experience
- **Competitive Advantage**: Premium capabilities without corporate intimidation factor
- **Target Value**: "C-suite intelligence with your best friend's personality"
